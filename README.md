## üèóÔ∏è Architecture Overview

WIRTHFORGE is a **local-first AI platform** that provides:

1. **Energy Visualization** - Real-time visualization of AI token generation as energy flows
2. **Multi-Model Orchestration** - Coordinate multiple local AI models (Turbo/Ensemble mode)
3. **Gamified Experience** - Progressive levels and achievement system
4. **Web-Engaged Interface** - Rich browser UI while keeping computation local
5. **60Hz Performance** - Real-time energy visualization at 60 FPS

## üéØ Core Principles

- **Local-First**: All AI computation happens on your device
- **Energy-Truth**: Visualizations based on real measured signals, no fake effects
- **Web-Engaged**: Rich browser interface for accessibility and collaboration
- **No Docker**: Native integration with local AI runtimes
- **Privacy Preservation**: Your data never leaves your device
- **Scientific Honesty**: Evidence mode reveals the metrics behind every visualization
